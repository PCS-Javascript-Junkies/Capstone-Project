About Choose Your Own Portland Adventure

"Choose Your Own Portland Adventure" was developed as a capstone project for the Portland Code Schools Full Stack Javascript Program. Our goal was to integrate our newfound skills into a fun and interactive web applicaiton that was not only challanging to develop but represented the culmination of our work thus far.
